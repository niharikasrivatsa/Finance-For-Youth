Saving

Saving means putting aside money for future use. A saving plan is a systematic or regular way to reach a financial goal. People save money for emergency needs such as a sudden furnace failure. We also save for short-term and long-term goals such as going to university or a house. Lastly, we save for security and future needs. People have retirement funds that can provide for them when they retire.

Investing means committing money in order to gain a financial return You can invest in stocks, bonds, and Guaranteed Investment Certificates (GIC).
